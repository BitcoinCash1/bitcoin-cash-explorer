USE YOUR OWN SELF-HOSTED MEMPOOL EXPLORER

For maximum privacy, we recommend that you use your own self-hosted instance of BCH Explorer on your own hardware. You can easily install your own self-hosted instance of this website on a Raspberry Pi using a one-click installation method maintained by various Bitcoin Cash fullnode distributions such as Umbrel, RaspiBlitz, MyNode, and RoninDojo. See our project's GitLab page for more details about self-hosting this website. By using your own self-hosted instance you will have maximum security, privacy and freedom.

USING THIS WEBSITE

Out of respect for the Bitcoin Cash community, this Website does not use any first-party cookies, except to store your preferred language and theme settings (if any). However, we do use minimal first-party analytics and logging as needed for the operation of this Website, as follows:

  • We use basic webserver logging (Angie) for sysadmin purposes, which collects your IP address along with the requests you make. These logs are deleted after 30 days, and we do not share this data with any third-party.

  • We use a self-hosted statistics application (Grafana) for analytics purposes, which collects your IP address along with the requests you make. Our grafana instance is configured to respect your privacy by redacting your IP address and other methods, and we do not share this data with any third-party. To conceal your activity from our analytics, we recommend that you use a privacy protecting browser extension that blocks grafana from being loaded.
